Privacy Policy

Your health data should stay understandable and protected.

This Privacy Policy explains how AI BMI Cal handles information when you calculate BMI, save records, set goals, use reminders, or contact support.

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Information you provide

AI BMI Cal may ask for details such as height, weight, age, gender, BMI goal, preferred measurement units, and reminder settings so the app can calculate BMI and display trends. These details are used to provide the app's core features.

BMI records and local storage

Saved BMI records are intended to help you review your personal progress over time. Unless a feature clearly states otherwise, the app stores your calculation history and preferences on your device and does not require an account to use the basic BMI calculator.

How we use information

Information in the app is used to calculate BMI, show health categories, generate trend charts, display goal references, remember unit preferences, deliver reminders you enable, and respond to support requests.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, AI BMI Cal may use iOS notification permissions to remind you to check or update your BMI. You can change notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Third-party services

The app may be distributed through the Apple App Store, and Apple may process purchase, download, crash, or analytics information under Apple's own privacy terms. If future versions add analytics, cloud sync, or other external services, the policy will be updated to describe those services.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal health information. We may share information only when needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, protect rights and safety, or respond to a request you make through a support channel.

Data retention and deletion

BMI records stored on your device remain there until you delete them in the app, remove the app, or erase your device data. If you contact support, we may retain your message for as long as needed to handle the request and maintain support records.

Children's privacy

AI BMI Cal is not designed for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support channel, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Health information disclaimer

BMI is a general screening measurement and may not reflect individual health conditions, body composition, pregnancy, athletic build, or clinical risk. AI BMI Cal does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice. Please consult a qualified clinician for personal health decisions.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. When we do, we will revise the updated date on this page. Continued use of AI BMI Cal after an update means the revised policy applies.

Contact

For privacy questions, app issues, or data requests, contact the developer through the App Store listing for AI BMI Cal.
