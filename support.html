How can we help with AI BMI Cal?

Find answers to common questions about calculating BMI, saving records, changing units, setting goals, and using reminders.

Getting started

Open AI BMI Cal, enter your profile details, then adjust height and weight on the calculator screen to see your BMI instantly.

Records and trends

Tap Save Record after a calculation to add it to your history. The trend chart shows your BMI changes over time.

How is BMI calculated?

AI BMI Cal uses the standard BMI formula: weight divided by height squared. Metric and imperial units are converted automatically.

Can I switch between kg/cm and lb/ft?

Yes. Use the unit control in the app to switch measurement systems. Your BMI result updates with the selected units.

How do I set a BMI goal?

Go to Settings and update your goal. The app displays it as a reference line in the BMI trend chart.

Why are reminders not appearing?

Make sure notifications are enabled for AI BMI Cal in iOS Settings. You can also review reminder settings inside the app.

Is AI BMI Cal medical advice?

No. BMI is a screening measurement and does not replace professional medical advice. Please consult a qualified clinician for health decisions.
